This site maps every relevant place on Earth using open data from
**OpenStreetMap**. Every point you see on the [map](/map) was contributed by a
real person mapping the world.

## Where the data comes from

We query the OpenStreetMap **Overpass API**, tag each point with its country,
and store everything as a single static dataset the map loads in one go. No
database, no tracking — just a fast map.

## Want a place added?

Add it to [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org) — it will appear here
on the next dataset refresh.
